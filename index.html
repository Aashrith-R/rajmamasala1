KASHMIRI RAJMA MASALA


This is a simple and authentic Kashmiri Rajma Masala recipe.

Required ingredients

  • Kidney Beans - 150gms
  • Onion - 1 medium
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium
  • Green Chilli - 2 nos.
  • Cumin seeds - 1 Teaspoon
  • Chillipoweder - 1 teaspoon
  • Dhaniya Powder - 1 Tablespoon
  • Oil - 3 Tablespoon
  • Bay leaf - 1 leaf

Cooking Process

  • Soak the beans for 12 hours
  • Cook the soaked kidney beans in a pressure cooker along with teaspoon of salt and bayleaf
  • Heat 3 Tablespoon oil in a seperate pan and once its hot add cumin seeds, chopped onion and ginger garlic paste. Toss it for 5 to 10 mins until oil seperates.
  • Add chopped tomatos,green chilli, dhaniya powder, chili poweder and salt as per taste.
  • Once the gravy is cooked, add the cooked beans into it and let it cook for 10 mins
  • For garnish add chopped coriarnder

KASHMIRI RAJMA MASALA is Ready
